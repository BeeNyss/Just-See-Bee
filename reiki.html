About Reiki

Reiki is a Japanese healing technique that promotes relaxation, reduces stress, and encourages natural healing through the gentle transfer of universal life energy.

During a Reiki session, I channel healing energy through my hands to help restore balance to your body's energy centres. This gentle, non-invasive treatment works on physical, emotional, mental, and spiritual levels to promote overall wellbeing.

Reiki is deeply relaxing and can help with stress reduction, pain management, emotional healing, and spiritual growth. Many people experience a sense of peace, clarity, and renewed vitality after their session.
