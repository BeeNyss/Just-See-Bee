About Just See Bee

Hi, I'm Bee. Based on the beautiful Sunshine Coast. I work from The Mooloolaba Wellness Collective and also offer mobile wellness treatments that come to you—so you can relax and heal in the comfort of your own space.

I'm a fully qualified Kinesiologist (trained at the College of Kinesiology in Brisbane) and a certified Myofascial Referral/Release Therapist. Using a unique blend of healing modalities, I support your physical, emotional, and energetic well-being with a truly holistic approach.

Every session is personalised to meet your individual needs—whether you're seeking pain relief, emotional balance, or deeper body awareness. Many clients experience noticeable pain reduction and increased range of motion in under 15 minutes.

Let's work together to reconnect you with your body's natural ability to heal.
