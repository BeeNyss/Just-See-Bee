4. Cancellations & No-Shows

We understand that life is unpredictable. If you need to cancel or reschedule, please notify us as soon as possible.

  • More than 24 hours' notice: No charge. Your deposit will be refunded or transferred to your next booking.
  • Less than 24 hours' notice: Your 50% booking deposit will be forfeited as a cancellation fee.
  • No-shows or arrivals more than 15 minutes late: The full session fee will be charged and your deposit will be forfeited.

A 50% deposit is required at the time of booking to secure your appointment. Repeated cancellations at short notice may result in full prepayment being required for future bookings.
