About Hot and Cold Stone Treatments

Hot and Cold Stone Treatment is an ancient therapeutic practice that combines traditional Ayurvedic techniques with the soothing temperatures of heated & cooled crystals to provide deep relaxation and healing.

This treatment focuses on an area that needs muscle or fascial releasing - areas where we commonly hold tension from daily stress. The addition of smooth, temperature-controlled crystals enhances the therapeutic benefits by improving circulation and allowing muscles to relax more deeply and the fascia to glide freely.

The crystals, combined with skilled techniques, work to release tension, improve circulation, and promote a profound sense of relaxation that extends throughout your entire being.
