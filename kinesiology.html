About Kinesiology

Kinesiology is a holistic approach to health and wellness that uses muscle monitoring (muscle testing) to identify imbalances in the body's structural, chemical, and emotional elements.

This gentle, non-invasive technique helps to identify what your body needs to restore balance and optimal function. By accessing the body's healing intelligence, kinesiology can help address a wide range of health concerns and promote overall wellbeing.

During a session, I use specific muscle tests to communicate with your body's innate wisdom and identify areas that need attention, whether they be nutritional, structural, emotional, or energetic.
